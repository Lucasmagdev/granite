## POST E02 — Quartzite vs. Quartz (The Most Confused Pair)

**Format:** Carousel (5 slides)
**Objective:** Education. High save rate — people screenshot this to show family.

**Art Headline (Slide 1):**
> QUARTZITE ≠ QUARTZ.
> THEY SOUND THE SAME.
> THEY ARE COMPLETELY DIFFERENT.

**Slide 2 — Quartz:**
> QUARTZ (engineered)
> Man-made from ground stone + resin.
> Consistent pattern. Zero maintenance.
> Not heat-resistant. Can't go outdoors.

**Slide 3 — Quartzite:**
> QUARTZITE (natural)
> 100% natural stone from the earth.
> Often has marble-like veining.
> Heat resistant. Outdoor-friendly.
> Harder than marble and most granites.

**Slide 4 — Why it matters:**
> QUARTZ IS SOLD EVERYWHERE.
> QUARTZITE IS MISIDENTIFIED EVERYWHERE.
> Some suppliers sell quartzite as quartz.
> Ask to see the test certificate before you buy.

**Slide 5 — CTA:**
> WE CARRY BOTH.
> AND WE'LL TELL YOU EXACTLY WHAT YOU'RE GETTING.
> Come to the showroom.

**Caption:**
This is the most common confusion we see in the stone industry — and it costs homeowners real money.

Quartzite is natural. Quartz is engineered. The name difference is one letter. The difference in material, behavior, and price is massive.

We carry both and we'll explain exactly what you're looking at and touching. No jargon. No upsell.
