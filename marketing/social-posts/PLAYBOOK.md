# St. Joseph Granite — Instagram Content Playbook

## Brand Position

**Who we are:** Local stone fabricator with 20+ years in Massachusetts. Not a big box store. Not a middleman. We cut, fabricate, and install in-house.

**Who we're talking to:**
- Homeowners planning kitchen or bathroom remodel
- Contractors and builders in Greater Boston / MetroWest MA
- People who searched "granite countertops near me" and found 3 options — we need to be the obvious choice

**The emotional job:** Make them feel smart for choosing us before they even call.

---

## Voice & Tone

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| Specific ("20 years", "Bellingham MA", "slab selection") | Generic ("quality service", "dream kitchen") |
| Talk to pain ("your kitchen looks dated") | Talk about us first |
| Short, punchy sentences | Long paragraphs |
| Direct CTA ("call now", "come to the showroom") | Soft CTA ("reach out to learn more") |
| Teach something real | Filler tips |
| Name materials correctly (quartzite ≠ quartz) | Simplify to the point of being wrong |

**Tone in one word:** Confident.

---

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Primary:** Deep red `#8B0000` / `#A31515`
- **Secondary:** Matte black `#111111`
- **Accent:** Stone white `#F5F0EB`
- **Text on dark:** Off-white `#EDE8E3`
- **Highlight:** Raw concrete gray `#B0A9A0`

### Typography
- **Headline (art):** Heavy serif or condensed sans. Examples: Playfair Display Bold, Bebas Neue, Anton
- **Body caption:** Clean sans. Inter, DM Sans
- **Rule:** Headline LARGE on art. Caption SHORT. Never more than 6 words on the image itself.

### Photography Direction
- **Real slabs** in the showroom — not stock photos
- **Installation day** shots: tools, countertops being set, workers present
- **Before/after** with same angle, same lighting
- **Close-up texture** shots of granite, quartz, marble, quartzite surfaces
- **Showroom interior** — slabs standing up, natural light
- **Remnant pile** — raw, industrial, honest
- NO staged lifestyle photos with fake families
- NO cheesy AI renders
- NO perfect Instagram kitchens from Houzz

### Graphic Rules
- Dark overlay on photos when adding text
- Text left-aligned or centered — never right
- Red accent line or block for emphasis
- 1–2 fonts max per post
- Logo small bottom corner — don't let it dominate

## Formats

| Format | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Carousel (5–8 slides)** | Education, comparisons, step-by-step |
| **Static single** | Strong single-image hooks, announcements |
| **Reel (15–30s)** | Before/after, process, fast tips |
| **Story (3–5 frames)** | CTA, polls, time-sensitive offers, remnants in stock |

---

## Posting Cadence (suggestion)

| Day | Type | Funnel |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Carousel | Education |
| Wednesday | Reel or Static | Awareness |
| Friday | Static or Story | Conversion |
| Sunday | Story | Authority / Behind scenes |

## What We Never Say

- "Quality craftsmanship"
- "Dream kitchen"
- "We take pride in"
- "Serving the community"
- "Reach out to us today"
- "Competitive pricing"
- "Family-owned business" (say the facts: 20 years, local, Bellingham — let them conclude it)
